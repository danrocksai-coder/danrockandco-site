Case Study • Digital Product Concept

Artful Intent.

A creative digital product brand concept built around wellness tools, budgeting templates, affirmation art, and a simple lead-magnet strategy for future e-commerce growth.

Project Type

Digital product brand concept

Focus

Brand direction, product structure, wellness tools, and digital downloads

Tools

AI-assisted design, Shopify planning, digital product organization

Status

Concept and product system in development

The Idea

Artful Intent was created as a calm, creative brand direction for digital products that combine practical organization with emotional wellness. The goal was to build something that felt useful, visually grounded, and approachable for everyday people.

What Was Created

Brand Direction

The brand was shaped around a clean, boho-inspired style with warm, minimal visuals and a grounded wellness feel.

  • Brand name development
  • Logo direction and visual style exploration
  • Clean-boho aesthetic direction
  • Soft, practical, wellness-focused positioning

First Product Concept

The first product was structured as the Mind & Money Toolkit, a beginner-friendly digital bundle combining wellness reflection tools with practical financial organization.

  • Daily mood tracker concept
  • Weekly self-care planner concept
  • Universal monthly budget template concept
  • Spending tracker and debt planning worksheets
  • Affirmation art and mobile wallpaper bundle concept

Lead Magnet Strategy

A free 3-day self-care starter was planned as a simple entry point to help introduce visitors to the brand and build an email list over time.

  • Freebie offer planning
  • Low-friction lead capture concept
  • Future email list strategy
  • Path from free download to paid digital toolkit

Honest Notes

Artful Intent is presented as a developing digital product concept, not a fully scaled e-commerce brand.

The value of this project is in the early-stage strategy: identifying the product direction, shaping the brand, organizing the first bundle, and planning a simple customer path from free resource to paid download.

This case study shows practical creative development, not inflated claims about sales, traffic, or revenue.

What This Shows

Artful Intent demonstrates the ability to take a broad creative idea and turn it into a structured digital product concept with brand direction, product packaging, pricing logic, and a future funnel strategy.
